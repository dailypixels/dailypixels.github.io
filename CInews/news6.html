  • localize stories for international markets

Micro-Publishing Is the New Bestseller Strategy

2025 has introduced a major shift:

Instead of releasing long novels or exclusive audio series, creators now publish:

  • short serialized chapters
  • daily micro-stories

  • AI-enhanced visual summaries
