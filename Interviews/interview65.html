Babar Azam — The Art of Staying Grounded

April 2, 2025 • By Adam Khan

interview Babar Azam cricket Pakistan leadership

Babar Azam has become one of the finest batsmen of modern cricket, known for elegance, calmness, and unstoppable consistency. From a ball-picker in Lahore to a global cricket star, his rise reflects patience, belief, and endless hard work.

Q1 — Your batting style is admired worldwide. What shaped your technique?

Babar: Hours and hours in the nets. I grew up watching heroes like Inzamam-ul-Haq and Virat Kohli—but my uncle Kamran Akmal pushed me harder than anyone. Discipline shaped my style.

Q2 — How do you handle the pressure of expectations from millions?

Babar: Pressure is a privilege. When people expect from you, it means they believe in you. I stay focused—each ball, one step at a time.

Q3 — What does leadership mean to you?

Babar: A leader is calm, even when the world is loud. It’s not just about runs—it’s about lifting the team, trusting your players, and believing till the last moment.

Q4 — Critics come with fame. How do you stay grounded?

Babar: I look at where I started. I talk to family—they keep me real. At the end of the day, I’m still a student of the game.

Q5 — What’s next for Babar Azam?

Babar: More growth. More milestones for Pakistan. I want young cricketers to believe that dreams come true through patience and faith.

Meaning / Reflection

Babar Azam’s journey reflects humility—he rises without noise, leads without ego, and inspires without force. His success is built on consistency and quiet determination.

Key Takeaway: Greatness is not loud. It grows slowly—through patience, respect for the craft, and the courage to stay humble while shining.

— End of Interview —
