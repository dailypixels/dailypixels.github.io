Key Principles of Neurodesign

  • Natural Light & Circadian Alignment: Using sunlight or circadian-mimicking lighting to regulate sleep-wake cycles and mood.
  • Biophilic Design: Incorporating plants, natural materials, and textures to promote calm and connection with nature.
  • Color & Cognitive Impact: Applying colors that stimulate creativity, relaxation, or focus depending on room function.
  • Clutter-Free Layouts: Organizing spaces to minimize distractions, improve flow, and support productivity.
  • Acoustic Considerations: Soundproofing or ambient sound choices to enhance concentration and reduce stress.

Neurodesign principles can be applied to:

  • Living spaces to promote relaxation and stress reduction
  • Work environments for enhanced team productivity and well-being
  • Healthcare or therapy settings to create calming, restorative spaces
