Concept Overview

Key Principles of Community Design

  • Inclusive Public Amenities: Libraries, community centers, and playgrounds promote engagement and belonging.
  • Safe & Accessible Infrastructure: Proper lighting, traffic control, and pedestrian-friendly paths enhance security and comfort.
  • Socially Engaging Layouts: Courtyards, plazas, and shared communal areas increase opportunities for human connection.
