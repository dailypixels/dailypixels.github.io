1. Identify What Truly Matters
Simplifying starts with priorities. List your values, passions, and non-negotiables. Knowing what truly matters helps you say no to unnecessary obligations and focus energy on what brings fulfillment.

2. Declutter Your Physical Space

Overcommitting drains energy. Evaluate recurring activities, appointments, and responsibilities. Prioritize meaningful engagements, delegate tasks when possible, and leave space for rest and reflection.

9. Create Daily Reflection Time
Set aside a few minutes each day to reflect, journal, or meditate. This daily pause helps you evaluate priorities, notice patterns, and maintain alignment with your simplified life.
