The Lantern Festival

February 2, 2025 • Mei Lin

Spiritual Tradition Hope

Mei had attended the lantern festival every year with her grandmother. After her grandmother passed away, Mei almost skipped it, afraid the empty space beside her would hurt too much.

But when the night arrived, she found herself in the crowd, clutching a single lantern. Around her, families whispered wishes into the night before releasing their glowing prayers skyward.

Mei lit her lantern, whispering: *“Thank you for your love. Guide me still.”* As it rose, a warmth filled her heart, as though her grandmother’s presence drifted upward with it.

Watching thousands of lanterns float into the heavens, Mei realized: love never vanishes. It transforms—into light, into memory, into guidance that carries us forward.
