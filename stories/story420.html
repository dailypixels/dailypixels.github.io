The Empty Chair

March 1, 2025 • Amir Hassan

Microfiction Life Loss

Every Sunday, the chair at the head of the table remained empty. A silent witness to laughter, grief, and memories.

One morning, he placed a fresh flower on it. Not to fill the void, but to honor the life that had touched theirs. The emptiness remained, yet it felt sacred—a reminder that love persists even in absence.
