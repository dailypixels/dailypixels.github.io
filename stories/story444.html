Lanterns of the Mind

August 18, 2025 • Leena Karim

Poetry Reflective Thoughts Inspiration

Thoughts drift like lanterns, Floating into the dark unknown, Some bright, some dim, Each carrying a wish, a fear, a hope.

Mind’s rivers twist and turn, Reflecting stars of insight, Shadows of doubt, Yet always, the current moves forward.

Release each lantern carefully, Watch as it rises, Letting imagination guide, And wisdom light the path.

— End of Poem —
