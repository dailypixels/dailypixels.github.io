The Lost Note

January 30, 2025 • Amir Hassan

Microfiction Life Regret

He found the note tucked between the pages of an old book: *“Meet me where the sunlight hits the old oak.”*

Years had passed. He was no longer sure who wrote it or what he hoped to find. Yet, curiosity pulled him to the oak tree in the park. The sunlight danced through the leaves. No one was waiting. Only the memory of what could have been.

He smiled softly. Sometimes the note didn’t matter. Sometimes the journey itself—toward hope, toward love, toward reflection—was the message.
