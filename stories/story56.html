The Day My Dog Ordered Pizza

September 16, 2025 • Olivia Bennett

Comedy Funny Pets

It started with a simple mistake. I left my phone unlocked on the couch. My dog, Max, has a talent for pawing at screens. Usually, he just opens YouTube or takes 47 blurry selfies of his nose.

But that night, the universe had other plans. I heard a knock at the door thirty minutes later. There stood a delivery guy holding a large pepperoni pizza.

“That’ll be $18.75,” he said. I stared at him, then at Max— who was wagging his tail proudly, like he’d just invented fire.

Turns out Max had somehow hit my food app, tapped “reorder last,” and Face ID (with my sleepy face still in view) confirmed the purchase.

The best part? Max hates pepperoni. He just wanted the crust. So yes, I paid $18.75 for a dog to eat two crusts and leave me with an entire pizza.

I didn’t even complain. Honestly, he deserved it. From that day on, Max has been known in our neighborhood as “The Pizza Dog.”
