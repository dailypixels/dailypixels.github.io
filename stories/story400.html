Sunlit Bench

December 25, 2025 • Hana Malik

Uplifting Hope Connection

On a chilly morning, Yusuf sat on the sunlit bench, noticing the first warmth of winter’s sun. Across the path, an elderly woman smiled and offered him her scarf.

They shared stories, laughter, and hot tea. By the end of the day, both felt lighter, reminded that small gestures, shared moments, and sunlight on a bench could lift the heaviest hearts.
