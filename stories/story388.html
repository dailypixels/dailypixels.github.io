The Clockmaker’s Secret

November 5, 2025 • Interview by Rehan Malik

Interview Wisdom Craft

Rehan: What inspired you to become a clockmaker?

Mr. Karim: Time fascinates me. Every gear, every tick is a reminder that life moves forward, even if slowly. Patience is the true art.

Rehan: Any secret to your craft?

Mr. Karim: Listen. Not just to the clocks, but to life. Each clock has its rhythm, like people have theirs. Respect it, and it will run smoothly.

Rehan left the workshop with more than knowledge of clocks. He carried a lesson: listening deeply, and valuing patience, can harmonize even the most complicated rhythms of life.

— End of Interview —
