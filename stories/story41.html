The Coffee Catastrophe

September 9, 2025 • Alex Rivera

Comedy Slice of Life Lighthearted

Mondays are hard. But this Monday was out to destroy me. I had one goal: grab a latte before work. Simple, right? Wrong.

The coffee shop was packed, the line snaking past the pastry case. I finally ordered, juggling my phone, wallet, and an alarming sense of déjà vu that I’d drop something. My name was called. Success! Or so I thought.

As I reached for the cup, my elbow clipped a tray of muffins. The muffins toppled like slow-motion dominoes, and in my panic to catch them, I flung my coffee straight into the air. It landed—miraculously—on the counter. Upside down. Lid off.

Everyone froze. A barista gasped. I muttered an apology while mopping up what was now a caramel-scented crime scene. The guy behind me chuckled. “First day of the week, huh?” I laughed, because crying felt too dramatic.

The staff, saints that they are, remade my drink for free. I slunk out of there, muffin crumbs in my bag and pride on the floor. At least now I had a story for the office—and a reputation as *‘the latte launcher.’*
