Morning Hymn

July 21, 2025 • Zara Ahmed

Poetry Spiritual Hope Nature

Dawn unfolds in quiet whispers, Light spilling over hills and rivers. Breath of morning, soft and tender, Awakens hearts, ignites the giver.

Leaves shimmer with dew, a hymn unsung, Birds chorus, wings and voices strung. Spirit rises, renewed and clear, Every shadow dissolves in morning’s cheer.

Open eyes, embrace the day, Step forward where the light may play. Faith in moments, small yet true, The world awakens, and so do you.

— End of Poem —
