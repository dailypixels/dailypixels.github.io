The Cat Who Stole My Sandwich

September 15, 2025 • Daniel Brooks

Funny Animals Lighthearted

Lunchtime was sacred. I had built the perfect sandwich— layers of turkey, crisp lettuce, and just enough mustard. I placed it on the table, turned for a drink of water, and when I came back… it was gone.

In its place sat Mr. Whiskers, the neighborhood stray cat, licking his paws with the confidence of a king. I blinked. He blinked back. It was less of a theft and more of a royal decree.

I tried bargaining. “Come on, buddy, half for me, half for you.” He yawned. Then, to rub salt in the wound, he dragged the sandwich under the porch and disappeared.

For days, I thought I’d never forgive him. But the next afternoon, he returned— not with my sandwich, but with a dead leaf placed neatly at my doorstep. A peace offering, perhaps?

From then on, I made two sandwiches: one for me, one for Mr. Whiskers. Peace was restored.
