Beyond the Horizon

December 4, 2025 • Saad Mehmood

Interview Exploration Adventure

Saad Mehmood interviewed Tariq Khan, a renowned explorer who crossed remote regions of the Arctic and Amazon. Tariq shared the challenges, dangers, and revelations that came with venturing into places few have seen.

“Exploration is as much inward as it is outward,” Tariq explained. Facing extreme conditions, isolation, and uncertainty teaches self-reliance, patience, and awareness of the interconnectedness of all life. He highlighted how documenting discoveries responsibly contributes to knowledge without harming ecosystems or communities.

Through the interview, readers gained insight into practical survival strategies, the psychology of adventure, and the philosophy of mindful travel. Tariq’s experiences emphasized curiosity, humility, and the importance of learning from the land and its inhabitants.

By the conclusion, the audience felt inspired to pursue their own journeys — literal or metaphorical — recognizing that true discovery requires courage, preparation, and respect for the world beyond our immediate view.
