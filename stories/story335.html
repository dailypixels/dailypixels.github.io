Whispers at Dawn

July 6, 2025 • Aamir Qureshi

Spiritual Peace Reflection

Leila rose before dawn to sit beside the mountain river. When the world was silent, she believed the universe whispered its secrets. But after her father passed, grief deafened her heart.

One morning, she broke down, asking the misty horizon: “Why do we lose those we love?” At that moment, a flock of birds lifted into the golden sunrise, and she felt something stir within.

The answer wasn’t in words but in presence. She understood that her father’s love had not vanished—it had transformed, flowing through memory like the river itself.

From then on, dawn was no longer a lonely silence. It was a sacred conversation with eternity.
