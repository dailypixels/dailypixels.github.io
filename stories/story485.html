The Locked Drawer

May 4, 2025 • Helena Varga

Mystery Secrets Thriller

Clara inherited her grandmother’s desk—oak polished smooth by decades of use. Every drawer slid open except one, a narrow one just above the knee space. It rattled but would not budge.

One evening, impatient, she pried at the lock with a knife. The drawer sprang open. Inside lay a faded photograph of her grandmother with a stranger, smiling beneath a banner that read: Prague Resistance, 1943.

Beneath the photo rested a pistol, oiled and ready, though untouched by dust.

Clara froze. The desk had not only carried family letters—it had carried a secret war.
