The Light Within

December 5, 2025 • Farah Siddiqui

Spiritual Faith Inner Peace

Karim had wandered through years of doubt and despair, searching for meaning in every book and sermon he could find. One morning, he sat quietly by the river, watching the sunrise.

He closed his eyes and listened—not to words, but to the rhythm of his heartbeat. Slowly, he felt warmth, calm, and a light rising from within, brighter than any outer guidance.

Karim understood: faith isn’t always in temples or texts. Sometimes, it’s in the stillness, in the quiet acceptance that the light you seek has always been inside.
