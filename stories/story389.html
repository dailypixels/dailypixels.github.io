A Letter to Tomorrow

November 10, 2025 • Areeba Khan

Uplifting Hope Courage

Layla wrote a letter addressed to her future self. She listed dreams, small victories, and the courage she hoped to carry.

She sealed it and placed it in the mailbox, smiling at the thought that tomorrow, she might be braver than today, stronger than yesterday.

That simple act reminded her that hope isn’t abstract—it lives in intentions, in small steps, and the belief that every day holds potential for joy and growth.
