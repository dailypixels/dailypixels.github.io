Wings of Morning

November 3, 2025 • Layla Chowdhury

Poetry Inspirational Hope Nature

Dawn rises on silver wings, Brushing the earth with light. Shadows retreat, the night dissolves, And hope unfurls with each breath.

Birds take flight, fearless, Teaching hearts that courage is motion, That every leap carries promise, And every morning, no matter how dark, Brings the chance to soar.

Step forward, listen to the wind, Trust the currents unseen. Life moves with quiet insistence, And even in uncertainty, Wings of morning lift us higher, Into clarity, light, and endless possibility.

— End of Poem —
