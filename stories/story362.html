Shadows and Light

July 1, 2025 • Naila Rahman

Poetry Hope Reflection

Between the cracks of dawn and dusk, Shadows linger where light must pass. Every broken path holds a story, Every sorrow blooms in silent glory.

We chase the sun yet embrace the dark, Knowing both leave their mark. Hope is the gentle wind that moves, Even when unseen, it proves That life is stitched with shadows and light.

— End of Poem —
