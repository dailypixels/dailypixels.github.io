Dawn of the River

May 12, 2025 • Layla Chowdhury

Poetry Inspirational Nature Hope

Mist curls over the river, Softly whispering the night’s secrets. Dawn stretches, golden and gentle, Touching each ripple with promise.

Trees bow to the light, Birds awaken, singing courage into the sky. Water carries reflections of hope, And dreams that dared to stay afloat.

Every current tells a story, Every wave carries possibility. Stand at the river’s edge, breathe deep, And feel life’s quiet insistence: move forward, For even the darkest night Ends in radiant, unstoppable dawn.

— End of Poem —
