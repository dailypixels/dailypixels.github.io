Whispers in the Rain

August 2, 2025 • Laila Noor

Poetry Nature Reflection

Raindrops tap, a secret song, Telling stories, short yet long. Each one falls, a fleeting voice, Reminding hearts they still have choice.

Streets reflect the silver skies, Broken dreams and hopeful eyes. Umbrellas bloom like guarded shields, Yet love still lingers in the fields.

In whispered drops, the world is new, A softer shade, a gentler hue. The storm may pass, the night may wane, But hope remains—within the rain.

— End of Poem —
