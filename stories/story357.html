A Single Rose

June 18, 2025 • Hana Li

Poetry Love Nature

A single rose rests on the table, Crimson petals whispering tales. Of love once tender, now fragile, Of mornings kissed by soft light, And nights where shadows spoke in silence.

Each fold holds a memory, Each scent a longing. Yet even in stillness, it breathes, A gentle reminder that beauty exists, Even when fleeting.

— End of Poem —
