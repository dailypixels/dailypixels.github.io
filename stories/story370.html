Ocean Whispers

August 1, 2025 • Rina Patel

Poetry Nature Reflection

Waves hum secrets in twilight’s ear, Whispering stories only hearts hear. Salt-kissed breezes carry dreams, Flowing softly through sunlight beams.

The tide retreats, leaving traces behind, Echoes of moments, both cruel and kind. Listen closely, the ocean implores, Every ending opens new doors.

— End of Poem —
