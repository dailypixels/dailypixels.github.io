The Silent Prayer

May 28, 2025 • Yasmin Ali

Spiritual Faith Peace

Amal often struggled with words. Whenever she prayed aloud, her tongue tangled, her thoughts scattered. One evening, exhausted, she simply sat in silence, hands folded, heart open.

The quiet grew deep, wrapping around her like a warm blanket. She felt as though someone listened, even without words. For the first time in years, she wept—not in despair, but in release.

She realized prayer wasn’t about eloquence, but presence. A silent heart could speak louder than a thousand spoken words. Peace settled in her soul like dawn breaking over a dark night.
