Moonlight on Paper

November 22, 2025 • Leila Hassan

Poetry Reflection Night

Silver whispers on a quiet page, Moonlight sketches dreams untold. Ink flows where shadows play, Stories born in nights of gold.

Stars peer softly through the pane, Watching verses rise and fall. Every line a gentle refrain, Every word a quiet call.

— End of Poem —
