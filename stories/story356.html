The Balloon Seller

June 15, 2025 • Javier Morales

Uplifting Kindness Hope

Tomas walks the streets with his basket of balloons every evening. Children rush to him, pulling parents along, laughter filling the square. For Tomas, it isn’t about money—it’s about the joy that blooms in their faces.

One rainy day, he notices a girl sitting alone, staring at the puddles. Without a word, he hands her a yellow balloon. Her eyes light up; a small smile breaks through the gloom.

Tomas realizes that sometimes the smallest gesture—a balloon, a smile, a kind word—can lift someone out of darkness. By the end of the day, puddles are forgotten, and hearts feel lighter.
