Wings of Tomorrow

December 20, 2025 • Saad Riaz

Inspirational Dreams Perseverance

Alina had always dreamed of flying—literally and figuratively. Each paper airplane she crafted carried a wish, a hope, a fragment of courage.

Her friends laughed, but she persisted. Years later, standing at the edge of an airfield with a real glider, she realized dreams take time, effort, and belief. The wings of tomorrow are built today.
