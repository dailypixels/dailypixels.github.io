The Garden of Echoes

February 12, 2025 • Noor Fatima

Poetry Spiritual Reflection

Leaves whisper secrets, Shadows hold old songs. In the garden of echoes, Silence stretches long.

Footsteps on moss, Heartbeats among trees, Every breath a prayer, Every sigh a release.

Light dances slowly, Morning unfolds in gold, In this sacred space, The soul learns to hold.

Here, the world softens, Time becomes gentle, And we remember: Peace lives in the patient.

— End of Poem —
