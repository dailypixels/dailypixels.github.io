Bridges of Yesterday

December 21, 2025 • Ayesha Tariq

Drama Life Family

Ayesha revisits her childhood town and walks across the old bridge that connects past to present. Memories of laughter, loss, and growth flood back, revealing lessons about forgiveness and the passage of time.

Reconnecting with family and friends, she understands how relationships evolve and how letting go of grudges allows healing. The bridge becomes a symbol of continuity, connection, and reflection on life’s journey.

By the end, Ayesha embraces both the past and the present, finding peace in understanding that the bridges we cross shape who we become and the love we carry forward.
