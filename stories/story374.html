Sunflowers for Lila

August 18, 2025 • Meera Joshi

Uplifting Hope Kindness

Lila loved sunflowers—they reminded her of her grandmother, who always said: *“Even in shadows, the sunflowers find the light.”* When Lila moved to the city feeling lonely, her neighbor surprised her with a small bouquet.

That single act sparked a chain of kindness. Soon, neighbors exchanged flowers, stories, and smiles. Lila realized that hope often arrives quietly, through small gestures that remind the heart of warmth and connection.

By summer’s end, Lila’s window overflowed with sunflowers. Each bloom a testament: even in the darkest seasons, light—and love—always finds a way in.
