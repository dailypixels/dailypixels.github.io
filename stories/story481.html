Last Signal

April 6, 2025 • Omar Khalid

Microfiction Sci-Fi Loneliness

The astronaut floated in silence, tether severed, Earth shrinking into a blue dot.

His radio crackled one last time. “We can’t reach you anymore. Godspeed.”

He smiled, pressing the recorder in his suit. “At least the stars stayed.”

Then, drifting into endless dark, he whispered to no one: “Transmit.”
