An Interview with the Mountain

January 20, 2025 • Zain Raza

Interview Inspirational Nature

Q: Mountain, how do you remain so steadfast through storms?

A: Patience, young one. I do not move with every wind. I endure. I listen. Every storm teaches me, shapes me, but does not define me.

Q: And what about those who climb you? How should they face their fears?

A: Step by step. Fear is part of the journey. Each footfall is progress. Sometimes, the path is hidden, sometimes rocky—but the summit rewards courage and perseverance.

Q: What do you wish humans would understand?

A: That strength is not always shown by speed or might. True strength is quiet, consistent, and enduring. Listen to the world, and it will guide you.

By the end, Zain understood: the mountain was more than rock. It was wisdom, resilience, and humility embodied. And he left its shadow forever changed, carrying patience in his heart.
