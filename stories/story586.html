Vanishing Point

December 10, 2025 • Daniyal Shah

Mystery Suspense Crime

Daniyal investigates a series of disappearances in a fog-covered city. Each case seemed unrelated at first, but subtle patterns emerged — a single clue connecting the victims: a small symbol etched into nearby walls.

Following the trail, he encountered secret societies, coded messages, and hidden passages. The deeper he went, the more he realized that the city itself concealed secrets older than its modern streets, with layers of deception crafted over centuries.

The investigation tested his logic, intuition, and courage. The final revelation exposed a network orchestrating disappearances for political and financial gain, forcing Daniyal to navigate moral dilemmas and confront danger at every turn.

In the end, the city returned to its façade of normalcy, but Daniyal carried the knowledge of its hidden depths, understanding that some mysteries persist even when the surface appears calm.
