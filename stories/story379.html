The River Within

September 15, 2025 • Amina Farooq

Spiritual Meditation Inner Peace

Samira walked along the riverbank, letting the water’s gentle murmur guide her thoughts. Life had been chaotic—work, family, responsibilities—but the river reminded her that stillness exists even in motion.

Sitting on a smooth stone, she closed her eyes and imagined the river within herself. Each breath became a ripple, each heartbeat a wave. She realized that peace wasn’t something to chase—it was flowing inside her, waiting to be recognized.

Returning home, Samira carried the calm of the river in her steps. She understood that life’s storms couldn’t disturb what flows steadily within.
