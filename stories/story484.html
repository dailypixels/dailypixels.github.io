Lanterns on the River

April 27, 2025 • Mei Lin

Poetry Tradition Hope

Lanterns drift, a quiet sea, Each a wish, a memory.

Shadows bow, the river keeps, Dreams that sail while silence sleeps.

Light will fade, but not the flame, Carried forth by hope’s own name.

Rivers change, but what remains— Lanterns whisper through the chains.

— End of Poem —
