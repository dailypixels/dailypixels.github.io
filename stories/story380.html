Conversations with the Gardener

September 20, 2025 • Interview by Aliya Khan

Interview Wisdom Life Lessons

Aliya: How do you find so much patience with these plants?

Mr. Iqbal: Plants teach me that growth is silent. You water them, you care, you wait. Nature does the rest.

Aliya: What’s the most important lesson the garden has taught you?

Mr. Iqbal: Life is seasonal. Some things bloom, others rest. You can’t force growth, but you can nurture it.

Aliya: And your advice for people facing challenges?

Mr. Iqbal: Be like the soil—support, hold, absorb, and stay patient. Challenges pass, and growth always happens.

Sitting among the flowers, Aliya realized that wisdom isn’t always loud. Sometimes it is quiet, like sunlight on petals, shaping lives gently but surely.

— End of Interview —
