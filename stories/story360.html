The Last Note

June 25, 2025 • Omar Rizvi

Microfiction Music Farewell

On the empty stage, only a single sheet of music remained. She touched it lightly, remembering the concert that would never be. Five notes lingered in her memory, each a heartbeat, each a goodbye.

She played them once more, slowly, deliberately, letting the sound fade into the empty hall. Then she left. Outside, the wind carried the melody into the streets, unnoticed by the crowd, yet perfectly heard by those who mattered most.
