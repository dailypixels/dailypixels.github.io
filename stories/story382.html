Raindrops and Reverie

October 2, 2025 • Zara Iqbal

Poetry Rain Reflection

Rain taps gently on the windowpane, Each drop a memory, a refrain. Streets glisten, whispers call, In every puddle, I see it all.

Footsteps merge with silver streams, Life reflects in fleeting dreams. In this quiet, a heart finds rest, Raindrops and reverie doing their best.

— End of Poem —
