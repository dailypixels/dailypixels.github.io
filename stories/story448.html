Paper Cranes

September 15, 2025 • Amara Khan

Poetry Hope Healing Spiritual

Folded wings of paper, Carried on fingers, Whispering wishes, silent prayers, Hope wrapped in colored shapes.

Each crane rises, Not in flight, but in spirit, Teaching patience, healing, And that dreams, even fragile, Can soar.

Hang them high, let light touch, And feel the quiet insistence of life— That even in stillness, Hope moves us forward.

— End of Poem —
