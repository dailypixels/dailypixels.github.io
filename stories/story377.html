Moonlit Verses

September 2, 2025 • Safiya Khan

Poetry Night Reflection

The moon casts silver whispers, Across still waters, silent and deep. Shadows dance and stories stir, Secrets the night alone can keep.

Crickets hum a gentle hymn, Leaves tremble in the cooling breeze. Thoughts wander, hearts wander, Finding solace in quiet ease.

— End of Poem —
