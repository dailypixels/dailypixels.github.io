Five Words

June 2, 2025 • Amir Hassan

Microfiction Life Emotions

She left a note on the table. Five words only: *“Don’t wait. Live your life.”* He stared at it for hours, torn between anger and gratitude.

Then he packed his suitcase, booked a ticket, and stepped into the unknown—realizing sometimes the shortest messages carry the longest journeys.
