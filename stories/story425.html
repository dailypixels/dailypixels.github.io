Moonlit Sonata

April 5, 2025 • Layla Chowdhury

Poetry Inspirational Hope

Moonlight spills on ivory keys, Notes like whispers, soft and bright. Every chord a story, Every pause a breath of light.

Shadows dance, the night listens, Hearts awaken, fears subside. Music becomes the bridge Between longing and the tide.

In silence, hope persists, In darkness, courage sings. Moonlit sonata teaches us: Even small lights carry wings.

— End of Poem —
