Reflections in Glass

December 11, 2025 • Sana Raza

Drama Life Identity

Zara looked into the shop windows lining the busy street, noticing her reflection merging with the scenes outside. Each reflection reminded her of the roles she played, the expectations imposed, and the choices she had made in search of approval.

Over weeks, she journaled and reflected, peeling away layers of conformity to discover her true desires. Conversations with friends, mentors, and even strangers helped her understand that identity is not fixed but evolves with courage and introspection.

By embracing her authenticity, Zara learned to live intentionally, appreciating moments of solitude, joy, and vulnerability. The reflections in glass became metaphors for self-awareness, clarity, and transformation.
