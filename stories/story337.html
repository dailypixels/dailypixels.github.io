When Stars Listen

July 12, 2025 • Maya Patel

Poetry Dreamlike Nature

Some stories aren’t told in paragraphs. They’re whispered in starlight.

She sat by the lake, her notebook trembling. The night was heavy with silence. But above, the stars leaned closer, listening. She wrote:

“The night is a canvas, and sorrow, a fading brush. Stars remind me— even darkness can shine.”

Closing her notebook, she realized poetry was less about answers, and more about giving silence a voice.
