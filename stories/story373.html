A Single Step

August 14, 2025 • Nabeel Tariq

Microfiction Courage Life

He stood at the edge of the cliff, heart pounding. One step forward could change everything—or nothing at all. He closed his eyes, inhaled deeply, and moved his foot. One single step.

In that moment, fear met resolve. The world seemed larger, possibilities infinite. That single step didn’t solve everything, but it began the journey. Sometimes, a life changes not in leaps, but in quiet steps toward courage.
