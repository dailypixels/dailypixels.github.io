Chandni Raatein

November 11, 2025 • Aliya Khan

Urdu Romance Poetry

Chandni ki raat thi, aur Sara apni khidki ke paas baithi thi, chaand ki roshni mein khoye hue khayalon mein. Har lehraati hui dhadkan uske dil ko yaadon ke sahare jeeti hui mehsoos hoti thi. Shehar ki shor-gul door se sirf ek dhundhli goonj lag rahi thi.

Usne yaad kiya pehli mulaqat, wo pal jab aankhein milin aur lamhe ruki gaye. Mohabbat ka izhar shabnam ke jaise halka aur narmi se uske dil ko chhoo gaya. Raat bhar, Sara ne apne journal mein likha, jazbaat jo alfaaz se zyada gehre the, shayad sirf chand hi unhein mehsoos kar sakta tha.

Chandni ki roshni ne har gham ko dhundhla diya, aur ek nayi umeed jagayi. Mohabbat sirf milne ka naam nahi, balki mehsoos karne, samajhne, aur yaadon mein jeene ka bhi naam hai. Us raat, Sara ne chaand se wada kiya ke har mushkil mein roshni dhundhegi aur dil ke jazbaat ko hamesha mehfooz rakhegi.

— Kahani Ka Aakhri —
