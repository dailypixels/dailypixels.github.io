The Great Grocery Cart Escape

September 9, 2025 • Alex Rivera

Comedy Slice of Life Family

It all started with a wobbly shopping cart. You know the kind—the one that rattles loudly and insists on veering left even if you’re just trying to buy some milk.

My plan was simple: grab a few groceries, get out in fifteen minutes. But the cart had other plans. The moment I let go to pick up a box of cereal, it took off down the aisle like it was auditioning for the Indy 500.

I chased after it, dodging a display of stacked soup cans (which wobbled dangerously) and apologizing to an elderly lady who was now clutching her handbag like I was part of a heist. The cart made a sharp turn at frozen foods, nearly taking out a poor man choosing ice cream.

By the time I caught it, panting and slightly mortified, a group of amused employees had gathered, one holding up their phone, clearly recording my “race.” The crowd even clapped as I wheeled the rebellious cart back to my aisle of shame.

Moral of the story? Never underestimate a runaway grocery cart. And maybe, just maybe, bring a list next time so you’re not running through the store like you’re in a reality TV competition.
