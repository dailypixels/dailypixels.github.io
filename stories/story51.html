Whispers Beneath the Bridge

September 14, 2025 • Mia Carter

Thriller Mystery Suspense

The river was silent, except for the steady drip of water leaking from the old bridge’s beams. I should have been the only one there, but a low voice echoed beneath the arches— as if someone was speaking directly into the fog.

I leaned over the railing, squinting into the shadows. The whispers grew louder, fragments of sentences I couldn’t piece together. Then I saw it— a faint lantern glow moving slowly across the water, though no boat was there.

My phone buzzed in my pocket. A single message flashed across the screen: “Don’t answer them.”

The whispers stopped. And in their place, a laugh— soft at first, then rolling through the arches, surrounding me.

I turned to leave, but a figure was already standing at the other end of the bridge, blocking the way back to town.
