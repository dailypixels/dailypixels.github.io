Autumn Letters

January 3, 2025 • Serena Blake

Romance Emotional Reunion Love Story

Years after their college separation, Lily and Ethan had gone silent, life’s circumstances pulling them in different directions. One crisp autumn day, Lily received a letter without a return address—Ethan’s handwriting unmistakable.

The letter invited her to meet at the old oak tree where they first confessed their feelings. Her heart raced, memories flooding back: laughter, late-night walks, and promises whispered beneath the leaves.

When she arrived, Ethan was already there, holding a stack of letters they had written to each other but never sent. Tears and smiles mingled as they read them aloud, each word rekindling the love that had never truly faded.

Standing together beneath the fiery canopy, they realized that distance and time had only strengthened their bond. Lily reached for Ethan’s hand, whispering, “Some stories aren’t meant to end.” Ethan smiled, “And ours is just beginning again.”
