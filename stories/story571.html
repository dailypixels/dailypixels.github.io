Voices of Innovation

November 25, 2025 • Ayesha Tariq

Interview Innovation Entrepreneurship

Ayesha Tariq interviewed Samir Haque, a visionary entrepreneur whose startups in clean energy and smart technology had gained global recognition. The discussion focused not only on technical innovation but also on leadership, risk-taking, and ethical responsibility in business.

“Innovation is meaningless without impact,” Samir emphasized. He shared stories of failed prototypes, lessons learned from mistakes, and moments when persistence and collaboration turned ideas into transformative solutions. He also spoke candidly about the pressures of balancing profitability with societal benefit.

Samir highlighted how fostering a culture of curiosity and inclusivity within teams leads to breakthroughs that are sustainable and meaningful. He encouraged aspiring innovators to embrace ambiguity, value learning over perfection, and prioritize empathy alongside technical skill.

By the end of the interview, readers were left inspired, understanding that true innovation is not only about creating something new, but about improving lives, fostering collaboration, and embracing challenges with resilience and integrity.
