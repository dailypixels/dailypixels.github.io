The Locked Room

May 10, 2025 • Marcus Hale

Mystery Thriller Suspense

Detective Eliza Grant stood before the sealed room. No windows, only one locked door, and yet inside lay the body of a missing banker. No signs of forced entry. No footprints but his own. It was the kind of case detectives feared—the impossible crime.

As Eliza studied the walls, she noticed scratches near the floorboards. Behind them, a narrow passage had been plastered shut. Whoever killed the banker had sealed their escape and relied on the illusion of impossibility to hide their crime.

The revelation turned the investigation into a race against time. If the killer was clever enough to stage this, they wouldn’t stop at one victim. Eliza tightened her grip on her notebook—the case had only just begun.
