Candle in the Quiet

November 1, 2025 • Hina Shah

Spiritual Meditation Reflection

In a darkened room, she lit a single candle. Its small flame danced, casting shadows that moved like memories across the walls.

She closed her eyes, listening to the silence. Every flicker reminded her that even in darkness, there is light, and presence matters more than words.

That quiet candle became a meditation on resilience and inner peace, a reminder that even a small spark can illuminate the soul.
