Whispers in a Teacup

April 15, 2025 • Layla Chowdhury

Poetry Peace Life

In porcelain stillness, steam ascends, Carrying whispers where silence bends. A sip of warmth, a breath of peace, In tiny moments, life finds release.

The world may roar with endless sound, Yet in this cup, calm is found. A single pause, a mindful art, Brewing patience within the heart.

— End of Poem —
