The Runner Who Fell

April 20, 2025 • Jonathan Wells

Uplifting Perseverance Sports

The crowd gasped when Sarah tripped on the track. Athletes blurred past as she sprawled on the ground, palms burning, knees scraped raw. Her medal hopes were gone.

She could’ve stayed there, listening to the roar fade. But something inside her whispered: Get up.

She rose, limping, and kept running. By the time she crossed the finish line, last place, the stadium was thunderous—not for the winners, but for her.

Sarah didn’t win a medal. She won something greater—the knowledge that falling doesn’t define you, rising does.
