Five Minutes Late

October 25, 2025 • Sana Tariq

Microfiction Life Fate

He arrived five minutes late. The café was empty. She had left a note: *“Some delays are meant to teach us patience.”*

Sitting alone, he realized that timing isn’t always ours to control, and small lapses can carry lessons larger than life itself.
