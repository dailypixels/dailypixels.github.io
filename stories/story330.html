The Vanishing Room

June 3, 2025 • Marcus Hale

Mystery Thriller Suspense

Detective Reyes had heard strange stories about the Baxter Hotel: Room 214 that appeared and disappeared at random. Guests swore they had stayed in it, only to return and find a blank wall where the door once was.

Determined to uncover the truth, Reyes booked a night. At 2 a.m., the door appeared. Inside, the room was ordinary—except for the journal on the desk, dated decades earlier, signed by people who had never checked out.

Suddenly, the walls pulsed as though alive. The exit vanished. The journal’s last line changed before his eyes: “Another has joined us.” Panic surged, but Reyes forced himself calm. He tore a page and shoved it into his pocket.

At dawn, the room was gone. He woke in the lobby chair, clutching the torn page. The journal’s words were still there—proof that the vanishing room wasn’t just a story, but a trap waiting for its next guest.
