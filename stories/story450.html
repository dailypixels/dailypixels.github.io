River of Dreams

September 29, 2025 • Layla Chowdhury

Poetry Inspirational Nature Hope

The river flows beneath the moon, Whispering secrets of lands unseen. Currents carry hopes like petals, Dreams folded in waves, waiting.

Stars emerge, reflecting in silver ripples, Guiding weary hearts toward possibilities. Each drop a story, each ripple a reminder That even in darkness, motion brings light.

Stand at the river’s edge, listen closely, And let it teach you: Courage is in the flow, Hope in persistence, And life, always, moves forward— Like a river of endless dreams.

— End of Poem —
