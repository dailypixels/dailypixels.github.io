The Lighthouse Keeper

March 28, 2025 • Imran Qureshi

Inspirational Spiritual Hope

Every night, Farooq lit the lighthouse. The storms were merciless, waves crashed like giants, but the light never faltered. Sailors relied on it, trusting its guidance without question.

One storm, Farooq’s lantern nearly went out. Trembling hands fought the wind, yet he pressed on. As dawn arrived, a ship safely reached the harbor, the crew waving in gratitude.

He realized life, like the lighthouse, was about steady persistence. Even when unseen or unacknowledged, consistency and faith provide guidance, comfort, and hope to those navigating darkness.
