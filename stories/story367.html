The Note on the Wind

July 20, 2025 • Arman Khalid

Microfiction Life Hope

A folded note flew out of her hands, caught by the wind, twisting and dancing across the park. She held her breath, watching it drift. *“You are stronger than you think,”* it read in her handwriting. Somewhere, someone else might find it.

Days later, she received a message from a stranger: *“Your note found me when I needed it most. Thank you.”* And just like that, she realized small gestures carried ripples far beyond what the eye could see.
